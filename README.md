# About the Project

Supply chains constantly face disruptions caused by weather events, port congestion, geopolitical conflicts, transportation delays and supplier issues.

**SentinelChain AI** continuously monitors multiple data sources, analyzes their impact using AI, and recommends proactive actions before disruptions affect business operations.

Instead of reacting after delays occur, businesses receive intelligent recommendations in advance.

---

# Problem Statement

Traditional supply chain monitoring is reactive.

Managers often discover disruptions only after deliveries are delayed or suppliers fail.

This leads to:

* Increased operational costs
* Inventory shortages
* Delivery delays
* Poor decision making
* Revenue loss

---

# Our Solution

SentinelChain AI continuously:

* Monitors global events
* Detects potential disruptions
* Evaluates business impact
* Recommends mitigation strategies
* Notifies stakeholders instantly
